Tuna Salad Sandwich

Home Page

Description:

Make a classic tuna sandwich for lunch.

To turn it into a tuna melt, simply toast the bread, then top one with cheese and grill until bubbling.

Ingredients:

  • 145g can tuna
  • ½ stick of celery
  • ½ small red onion
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • small pinch of paprika
  • 4 slices granary bread
  • 8 slices cucumber
  • 4 Little Gem
  • 2 tsp mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. To make the tuna salad tip all the ingredients into a bowl, season with a little salt and lots of pepper and use a fork to mash everything together until completely mixed.
  2. To assemble the sandwiches pile and spread the tuna salad over two slices of the bread.
  3. Add the cucumber and lettuce, then spread the remaining slices with the mayonnaise and use them to top the sandwiches.
  4. Press down on the sandwiches lightly, then halve (rectangles or triangles) and serve.
